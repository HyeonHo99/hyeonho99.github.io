About Me

I am a graduate student at KAIST AI, advised by Prof. Jong Chul Ye. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science at Sungkyunkwan University in Aug. 2023.

My research interests lies in Controllable Video Generation and Editing. I am also broadly interested in generative modeling across various modalities (image, video, 3D, 4D) using diffusion models.
Refer to my CV for details.

I will be joining Adobe Research London (full-time research engineer) this summer!
